About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional with over two years of hands-on experience defending organizations against evolving cyber threats. My expertise lies in Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring, SIEM administration, and incident response—detecting threats, investigating incidents, and implementing solutions that protect critical assets.

I specialize in 24/7 security event monitoring using enterprise SIEM platforms including Splunk Enterprise, IBM QRadar, and Securonix SNYPR. My work involves analyzing thousands of security events daily, correlating threat intelligence with network traffic, and responding rapidly to incidents. I've prevented significant financial losses by detecting live attacks and have consistently reduced organizational risk through proactive threat hunting.

I recently completed a Post-Graduate Certificate in Cyber Security and Computer Forensics from Lambton College (GPA: 3.53/4.0), where I earned Student of the Term honors. Combined with my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering with a major in Cyber Security from Ganpat University, I bring both academic excellence and practical expertise to every challenge.
